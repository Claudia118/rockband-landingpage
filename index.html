About The Band

Dead Inside are an English rock band from Teignmouth, Devon, formed in 1996. The band consists of Matt Williams (lead vocals, guitar, keyboards), Chris Allen (bass guitar, backing vocals), and Brooks Howard (drums).Dead Inside released their debut album, The Rise, in 1999, showcasing a melancholic alternative rock style.
